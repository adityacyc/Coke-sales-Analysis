# Coca-Cola Sales Analysis Using Excel

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Coca-Cola beverage sales data across major American retailers during 2021 using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to identify sales trends, retailer performance, regional demand patterns, and profitability drivers through data cleaning, pivot tables, charts, and dashboard creation.

## Business Problem

**How can Coca-Cola optimize sales and profitability across U.S. retailers?**

The analysis focuses on understanding:

* Top-performing retailers
* Best-selling beverage brands
* Regional sales performance
* Monthly sales trends
* Profitability and operating margins

## Dataset Summary

The dataset contains:

* Retailer information
* Invoice dates
* Region, State, and City
* Beverage Brand
* Price per Unit
* Units Sold
* Total Sales
* Operating Profit
* Operating Margin

### Dataset Size

* **3,888 records**
* **12 business columns**
* Sales data for the year **2021**

# Data Preparation

The following steps were performed:

* Checked for missing values
* Verified data types
* Standardized date format
* Created Month field for trend analysis
* Built Pivot Tables for multidimensional analysis
* Developed an interactive Excel Dashboard

# Charts & Visualizations Created

### 1. Total Sales by Retailer

**Purpose:** Compare retailer performance.

**Insight:**

* Sodapop generated the highest revenue among all retailers.
* DreamCo contributed the lowest sales volume.

---

### 2. Total Sales by Region

**Purpose:** Identify high-performing geographic markets.

**Insight:**

* West region recorded the highest sales.
* Midwest generated the lowest revenue among all regions.

---

### 3. Total Sales by Beverage Brand

**Purpose:** Understand product demand.

**Insight:**

* Coca-Cola is the best-selling beverage brand.
* Dasani Water and Diet Coke are also strong contributors.
* Fanta generated the lowest sales among the listed products.

---

### 4. Monthly Sales Trend

**Purpose:** Analyze seasonality and demand fluctuations.

**Insight:**

* December recorded the highest sales.
* July and August also showed strong performance.
* Sales generally increased during the second half of the year.

# Business Insights

### Retailer Performance

* Sodapop contributes more than 50% of total sales, making it Coca-Cola's most valuable retail partner.
* Increasing partnerships with high-performing retailers can further boost revenue.

### Regional Analysis

* The West region shows the strongest customer demand.
* Marketing and inventory investments should be prioritized in this region.

### Product Analysis

* Coca-Cola remains the flagship revenue driver.
* Dasani Water and Diet Coke have strong market acceptance and present growth opportunities.

### Seasonal Trends

* Sales peak during holiday and summer seasons.
* Demand forecasting can help optimize inventory and distribution planning.

## Conclusion

The analysis reveals that retailer concentration, regional demand, and product popularity significantly impact Coca-Cola's sales performance. By focusing on high-performing retailers, strengthening operations in the West region, and leveraging seasonal demand patterns, Coca-Cola can improve both revenue and profitability.
